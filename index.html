About Me

Hello! I’m Alhan Ali, currently preparing to enter Grade 11. I have a deep passion for learning, especially in Systems Design & Technology and Math — my two favourite subjects that inspire me to think creatively and solve complex problems every day.

I was born in Karachi, Pakistan, a vibrant city rich in culture and history. Over the years, I’ve been lucky enough to travel to incredible places including Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Toronto, Edmonton, and Calgary, each visit broadening my perspectives and fueling my curiosity about the world.

Outside academics, I am a die-hard Pakistan cricket fan, and cricket is my favourite sport. Whether following international matches or playing with friends, cricket teaches me teamwork, focus, and resilience — values I carry into everything I do.

This website showcases my journey through volunteering, projects, achievements, and hobbies. Feel free to explore and reach out!
